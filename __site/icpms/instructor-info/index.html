This experiment requires the following items.

Supplies

  • use 25 ml vol flask

  • use 25 ml pipette

  • stir rods

  • cuvettes

  • beakers, 50 ml

  • KimWipes (small)

Reagents

  • 20 ppm phosphate stock solutions (make sure to record the exact concentration in your notebook!)
