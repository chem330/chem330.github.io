Nitrate Instructor / Prep Information

This experiment requires the following items.

Supplies

  • use 25 ml vol flask

  • use 25 ml pipette

  • stir rods

  • cuvettes

  • beakers, 50 ml

  • KimWipes (small)

Reagents

  • 500 mg/L nitrate stock - prepared from sodium nitrate that has been dried in an oven.

  • NitraVer 5 packs
