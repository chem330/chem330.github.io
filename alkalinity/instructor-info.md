Alkalinity Titration Instructor / Prep Information
=======================================
 
This experiment requires the following items.

# Supplies

- Burettes
- Stir plates
- Stir bars
- Erlenmeyer flasks
- pH paper
- pH calibration buffers
- pH probes

# Reagents

- Sodium carbonate (0.05 N)
- H2SO4 or HCl (0.1 N)
- H2SO4 or HCl (0.02 N)
